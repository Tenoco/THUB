Silicon Valley Debates AI Weapons' Autonomy

A growing debate in Silicon Valley centers around the use of fully autonomous AI weapons. While some tech leaders, like Shield AI's Brandon Tseng, oppose such weapons, others, including Anduril's Palmer Luckey and Palantir's Joe Lonsdale, show openness to the idea. The discussion highlights the complex ethical and strategic considerations surrounding AI in warfare.
